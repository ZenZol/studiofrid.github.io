APPOINTMENTS
You can make an appointment with Studio Frid in a number
of ways including via our website, telephone, text or email.
Upon making a booking, you will receive an email
confirming all the important details, along with an email and
sms reminder 25 hours prior to your appointment.
If this is your first appointment with us, you will have to
complete a Client Intake Form before your appointment or
in the beginning of your appointment with the help of the
practitioner.
CANCELLATION
We require a minimum of 24 hours notice to cancel an
appointment. Failure to attend an appointment with less
than 24 hours notice of cancellation will incur the charge for
the full appointment. This is because we may not be able to
fill your appointment slot and still need to cover our costs.
Please cancel as soon as possible if a foreseeable event is
likely to prevent you from attending, for example, illness or
adverse weather conditions. An Vipps request or an incvoice
will be sent to you for any late cancellation or ‘no show’
charges. Following a cancellation, payment must be
received before any further massage bookings can be taken.
LATE ARRIVALS
It is important that our agreed appointment time and
duration is adhered to. We appreciate that sometimes,
things happen, and you may arrive a little later or need to
leave a bit earlier than planned. This is fine, but we cannot
guarantee to make up the missed time by overrunning or
adding time to subsequent appointments.
CONFIDENTIALITY
All client information and records will be safeguarded by
Studio Frid and will remain confidential. Unless required to
do so by law, we will not otherwise share, sell or distribute
any of the information you provide to us without your
consent.
Consultations and client treatment details will not be
discussed with anyone other than the client, unless the
client is under the age of 18 or has a care worker or
guardian.
In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, we
employ security measures to protect your information from
access by unauthorised persons and against unlawful
processing, accidental loss, destruction and damage. We
have also put in place suitable physical, electronic and
managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the
information we collect online. We will retain your
information for a reasonable period or as long as the law
requires.
Each practitioner working for Studio Frid will have access to
these records only for the duration of their contract with
Studio Frid, and solely for the purpose of providing you with
the most appropriate treatment.
For more information, please review our Privacy Policy.
HEALTH & SAFETY
Due to the nature of massage and the need for close
contact, all Studio Frid practitioners will maintain a high
standard of personal hygiene and clients are asked to
ensure that they also maintain a high standard of personal
hygiene.
If a practitioner is unwell, suffering from a cold or virus,
infection or general ill health, they will not be allowed to
work due to the risks of cross contamination. This may
result in Studio Frid contacting you to inform you of the
issue at the earliest opportunity to make suitable alternative
arrangements.
Clients are asked not to attend for a massage if they are
unwell, suffering from a cold or virus, infection or general ill
health. Please contact Studio Frid via email at
post@StudioFrid.no at the earliest opportunity to rearrange
your appointment. If you are unwell and contact us to
inform us of your illness, please note that our Cancellation
Policy may still apply.
Studio Frid, including the practitioners, will not be held
responsible for the loss or damage of personal items during
your time at the Studio Frid premises.
Clients under the age of 18 years must be accompanied by
a parent or guardian. This parent or guardian must give
permission for the agreed massage plan to be performed.
They will be invited to stay in the room for the duration of
the appointment, and expected to remain present
throughout the treatment.
Studio Frid holds the right to refuse any treatment if a client
is physically or verbally abusive towards any practitioner, or
demonstrates inappropriate, aggressive or sexual behaviour.
Such behaviour may result in a partial or total ban from
Studio Frid, and may also result in criminal proceedings.
